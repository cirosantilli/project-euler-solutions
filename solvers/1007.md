For a fixed parenthesization, the coefficient of a leaf is `-1` to the power of
the number of right turns on its path from the root.  If `P_n(y)` is the sum,
over all Catalan parenthesizations with `n` minus signs, of these signed leaf
polynomials, then splitting at the root gives

`P(x, y) = 1 / (1 - x C(x) + x y C(x y))`,

where `C(x)` is the Catalan generating function.

The desired sequence is obtained by evaluating the leaf polynomial on Fibonacci
numbers:

`A(n) = (P_n(alpha) - P_n(beta)) / (alpha - beta)`,

where `alpha` and `beta` are the two roots of `t^2 - t - 1`.  This is an
algebraic generating function, so its coefficients satisfy a polynomial
recurrence.  The solver uses that recurrence modulo `10^9 + 9`, with the given
small cases as assertions.
